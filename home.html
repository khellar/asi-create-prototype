A real mind, not a wrapper

A neural-symbolic mind you can watch think.

Most agents are an LLM on a loop. Yours runs a real neural-symbolic mind — Hyperon · MeTTa · PLN/NARS — a “careful mind” that forms evidence-grounded beliefs, revises them as it learns, and reasons over what it knows. And because Hyperon is a living, open AGI stack in active development, your agent isn’t frozen: it inherits the substrate’s advances as the research moves forward. You don’t rewrite the algorithms — you watch them run, shape the mind they reason over, and grow with the stack.

Trustworthy — beliefs are evidence-grounded and inspectable, so it makes far less up; every decision is auditable.
It gets better — it remembers and reasons over what it knows, accumulating with evidence, not a goldfish context window.
Open & yours — an open AGI substrate you own and take with you, and a seat on the real AGI-research frontier — not a closed product.

What that means: you watch PLN, NARS and MeTTa fire, see beliefs form and revise, and edit your agent’s mind (prompt, self-model, memory). You can’t — and don’t need to — rewrite the core algorithms; that’s the substrate doing the hard part. And you can wire your own Claude Code or Codex into its files, editing while you chat.
